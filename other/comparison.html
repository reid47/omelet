About

Hey. I’m Reid. I’m a software engineer, web developer, filmmaker, and student at Pomona College in Claremont, CA. I love code, good design, movies, word games, and making things that will make other people’s lives a little easier or a little happier.

After years spent lurking around and learning from other developers’ sites, I felt it was time to give a little something back to the community that taught me so much. Hence, this site. I hope that there’s someone out there who can learn from what I post here. At the very least, I hope that I can start some interesting conversations. For that reason, all of the posts and projects on this site have a comments section. Please don’t hesitate to comment with questions, ideas, suggestions, etc. I’m still learning every day, and I’d love to hear what you have to say.

If you’d like to reach me directly, feel free to contact me.
